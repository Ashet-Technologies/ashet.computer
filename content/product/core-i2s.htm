Core I²S™

The Core I²S™ is our baseline audio communications core. Designed for cost-efficient designs, it enables you to create high-quality audio appliances for your customers.

Ashet Technologies™ is proud to provide you with this new technology and enables you to create consumer and industrial acustic appliances with an ease.

Features

  • Fixed audio sample rate of 22.05 kHz
  • Automatic mono-to-stereo conversion
  • High definition 16 bit sample depth
  • Wide support for I²S devices
