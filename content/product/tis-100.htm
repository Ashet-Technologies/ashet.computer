TIS 100™

The Tessellated Intelligence System™ is a system designed by Zachtronics. Ashet Technologies™ has licenced the TIS-100 system architecture and implemented a TIS-100 core you can use in your designs.

The TIS-100 is a highly decentralized system architecture, with simple, but powerful CPU cores.

Ashet Technologies™ is proud to provide you with this technology and enables you to create distributed systems at the snap of a finger.

Features

  • Fully compatible to the original specification by Zachtronics
  • Synthesisable for real hardware
  • Comes with a complete toolchain
