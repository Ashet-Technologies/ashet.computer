Magic Video 100™

The Magic Video 100™ is our baseline video processor. While designed to be cost efficient, it still delivers good video performance.

Ashet Technologies™ is proud to provide you with this new technology and enables you to create visual appliances with an ease.

Features

  • Fixed output resolution of 400x300 pixels
  • Dynamic virtual resolution between 1x1 and 400x300 pixels
  • 8 bit per pixel video buffer
  • 256 item hi-color palette (RGB565)
